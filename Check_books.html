Book List

  • To Kill a Mockingbird
    by Harper Lee
    A novel about the serious issues of rape and racial inequality, told through the eyes of young Scout Finch in the Deep South.
  • 1984
    by George Orwell
    A dystopian story set in a totalitarian society under constant surveillance, exploring themes of truth and freedom.
  • Pride and Prejudice
    by Jane Austen
    A classic romantic novel that delves into issues of class, marriage, and morality in 19th-century England.
  • The Great Gatsby
    by F. Scott Fitzgerald
    A critique of the American Dream, set in the Roaring Twenties, centered on the mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby.
  • The Hobbit
    by J.R.R. Tolkien
    A fantasy adventure following Bilbo Baggins as he embarks on a quest to reclaim a lost dwarf kingdom.
